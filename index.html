How to Play

To play, hit the ‘Play’ button and watch the random board pattern unfold! You can also pause and then click on any of the cells to add to the current board, or hit the ‘Clear’ button to start from scratch. Or you can hit ‘Refresh’ to get a completely new random board to play around with.

Try to get your pattern to play out indefinitely, see how quickly you can get to an empty board, or try some of these interesting pattern examples . Intrigued how the Game of Life works? Read all about it on the Wikipedia page .
